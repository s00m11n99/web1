Golden Child-금동이들

Korea's 10-member boy band, Woollim Entertainment, debuted on August 28, 2017.It is Woollim Entertainment's third idol in three years since Lovelyz and the second boy band in seven years since INFINITE. The group name Golden Child is "The Perfect Child with Only One Person in 100 Years," meaning to lead the Korean music industry for 100 years and lead the music trend for the next 100 years.

Since January 2017, the members have been unveiled through W Project, a debut launching project, and in May of the same year, they first greeted the public with their debut reality "2017 Woollim PICK." It also ranked eighth in the 2017 K-Pop rookie group selected by Billboard and fifth in this year's (2017) by Idology.
